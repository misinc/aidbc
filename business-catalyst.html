What is Adobe® Business Catalyst?

Adobe’s Business Catalyst is the best all-in-one platform for building online businesses. BC includes all the tools you need to run an online business.

Flexible Content Management System (CMS)

With BusinessCatalyst you can use any HTML/CSS code you want. You’re only limited by your imagination and your coding skills!

Online Store (e-commerce)

Easily sell products online, whether they are physical items or electronic downloads and BC will handle all the complexities.

Web Apps

No need to rely on flaky third party plug-ins that may or not work. BC includes a wide range of useful modules that can help you manage your online business.

Customer Database and Web Forms (CRM)

BC includes a built-in customer database, so each time your clients contact you using one of your web forms their data will be safely and securely stored in your CRM. From there you can see their order history, add them to a mailing list or run reports.

Reporting and Analytics

Use the built-in reports or create your own custom reports and see the stats for your online business in one place. No need to go to multiple places to see your website stats. All the stats are in one place and if you’re busy you get a weekly summary of your stats by email.

Email Marketing

You can build up multiple mailing lists and send out targeted email campaigns easily and track the results.
