Welcome to BC Notifications

This app allows you to easily setup Notifications when purchases or inquiries are made on your website. The notifications will appear as push notifications on your mobile device (iPhone and Android). If you use Safari, you will also be able to receive notifications in your browser. This app requires Boxcar, which is a free app and service available on the App Store and Google Play.
